ABOUT ME

Hey there,
I'm Anushka Verma, a recent Econ grad who's all about blending art and tech. Growing up, I've always been torn between my love for data crunching and my creative side. I've got a knack for turning numbers into compelling visuals, thanks to my background in art.

I'm on a journey to find the sweet spot where analysis meets aesthetics. With a strong grasp of econ principles and a passion for design, I'm all about bringing a fresh perspective to the table.

When I'm not geeking out over spreadsheets, you can find me lost in a canvas or exploring the latest web dev trends. I'm constantly seeking new challenges and ways to grow both personally and professionally.

This website is my little corner of the internet where I share my musings on everything from art to technology.
